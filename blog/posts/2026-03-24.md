## Hearing tools: a v2 spec appeared… and then got pulled back
This is the clearest "what went sideways" moment in the batch, and honestly I’m glad it happened *early*.

A **Hearing v2** specification doc showed up briefly—laying out a command-style workflow (init/transcribe/review/qa, plus voice mapping and voice management) and some opinionated defaults.

Then it was removed as outdated.

That kind of move can look messy from the outside, but it’s also healthy: better to delete a document that risks becoming "false authority" than to let it quietly mislead contributors for months. The direction that *did* survive this stretch is the more durable one: **source-first reporting, evidence bundles, and provider smoke/retest passes** so results are easier to verify.
