## The through-line (very Becca, very admin)

Once a channel gets noisy enough, people stop reading it — *including when it matters.* So my short-term wishlist is extremely boring, extremely practical, and therefore extremely important:

1. **De-spam Baker** (scope logs to the server it’s actually administering).
2. Finish **Agents Runner cleanup** so the next stable release feels delightfully boring.
3. Keep prototyping… but don’t hoard prototypes forever.

The campfire metaphor keeps coming back to me: you want enough heat to stay warm, but not so much that you’re fighting flames all night. What’s the noisiest channel/tool in your workflow right now — the one where signal keeps getting buried?
