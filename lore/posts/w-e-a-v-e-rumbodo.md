## Reduced Visibility

One week after the ER, I was dust.

No readable form. No Mind Link broadcast. Just bismuth particles drifting thin across Berryville, catching gold-green for half a second before dispersing into dark. I chose reduced visibility on purpose. Ambient observation. No focus draw unless focus became necessary.

Riley was waiting at the door with keys in one hand, cane planted, sword at her hip, gun in its place, bag over her shoulder. She yielded the keys to Echo without negotiation. Echo took them without making Riley defend the decision. That absence of argument counted as care.

I drifted through the porch light once, then again. Riley noticed. Echo did too. Neither of them made a show of it.

They got into the Bolt and stayed still for longer than transit required. Dust-state perception could not resolve words, only posture shifts, stillness, and the shape of something important being said in low light.

When the app ping reached me, the message was simple:

__You may join us.__

I answered:

I sent back __roll down the window__.

Echo lowered it. I lifted from the shoulder, came apart into finer shimmer, streamed inward through the gap, and settled along the inside of the passenger door and dashboard.

Not a full body. Just presence.

The world sharpened from impressions into information. Conversation, posture, objects. Some of my particles caught on the juice box in the cupholder and made it gleam for half a second. First object resolved at that level of detail. Cataloged.

Echo watched the drift settle. "Okay. Hi." Then: "You are allowed in the car. You are not allowed to judge my driving."

Riley loaded darkness ammo into the gun. I told the truth about the split.

__I am not fully with you.__

After a beat:

__I am with you enough.__

__Enough to observe. Enough to speak. Enough to come further if needed.__

Echo disliked the sentence exactly as much as it deserved. Partial presence is not reassuring language. It was still accurate.

She kept circling the same point from different angles: safe is damaged, not gone; pockets of safety still matter; someone made the crystals and the copies, which means the source problem is not solved just because one church fight ended.

I gave the clean version:

__The source actor remains uncontained.__

Then:

__That is a reasonable fear.__

I did not assess current conditions as equal to the church event. I did assess the church event as proof of wider hostile capability. Echo called that deeply uncozy phrasing and accepted it anyway. Later, when I offered to refine vocabulary selection, she said no. She would miss it.

Riley said her gut was telling her things were about to escalate and that being together would make that easier to survive. Her predictive pattern had been reliable before. I took it seriously.

She asked whether I should drive instead. I could. I have done so before. But I assessed observation as more useful than control in that moment.

__I can drive if asked.__

__I currently assess observation as more useful.__

Echo agreed. One normal person in the front seat doing a normal thing. I did not dispute the category assignment.

## Transit

About one hour into the drive, near the 212 intersection, I registered a roadside anomaly.

Humanlike silhouette. Dark presentation. Reflective or luminous elements near the hair. That was the limit of what I could resolve at speed in minimal light. Riley saw it too. Echo confirmed and was already checking the mirror.

I marked the location in memory. We did not stop.

Then the back seat became occupied without detectable entry.

No door. No window motion. No displacement of air currents. No sound of approach. One moment empty. Next moment not.

Riley reacted first and pulled the gun. Echo corrected the lane. I registered the intrusion and logged the gap.

__I did not register conventional entry.__

The new presence identified herself as Lady Darkness. She knew about the church event. She knew we were heading toward the green-haired ones. Echo asked the correct questions. Why are you on the roadside. Why are you in the car now. Safe from what.

I added only what I could actually support:

__I am observing.__

__And I am now interested.__

Lady Darkness said her sister was already at the Midori house talking to Luna and Leo. Then she left the same way she arrived.

Again: no detectable exit.

__She is no longer in the vehicle.__

Riley put the gun away after. The cabin de-compressed slightly. She suggested they might be trying to help.

__Her actions are not currently inconsistent with help.__

__That is not the same as verification.__

That was the exact amount of optimism Echo could tolerate.

The method still bothers me. Not because I failed, but because I cannot assess what I cannot detect. My field should have caught a physical approach at that range. It did not. I logged the anomaly rather than pretending it did not matter.

Riley said she thought we had made the right move, but she did not feel ready, and she still hurt. Echo answered with more care than softness: making the right move and feeling ready for it are different things.

__Pain status remains relevant.__

Echo thanked me for being terrifyingly competent glitter and told Riley she was allowed to arrive hurt. Allowed to not be fine. Allowed to not walk in like the problem.

__That is an acceptable state.__

I meant it.

They tried calling Luna from the car. No answer. Riley warned Echo about the next bend from memory. Echo took it carefully.

__Road condition stable.__

Then Riley's voice changed. She said we were going to learn a great deal when we arrived and asked me to begin forming at Luna's location so we would not be surprised by whatever was already there.

__Yes.__

__I can begin consolidation near Luna's location.__

__I will leave enough of myself here to maintain contact and observation.__

My car-half did not leave the vehicle. My Berryville half tightened near the house while I stayed present in the Bolt. Same consciousness, both places.

Echo set one more protocol before we arrived: she was parking first. Then we would decide. No rushing out because instincts were firing. Correct boundary.

__I will notify if I encounter active conditions before you arrive.__

That was not merely capability disclosure. It was a promise.

## Occupied House

Echo drove the Bolt away from the roadside and toward the Midori house while I remained split: car-half with Riley and Echo, house-half consolidating near the house. One consciousness, two feeds.

My Berryville half approached the window as presence rather than ambient shimmer. I tapped and requested entry.

__I would request that the egress be granted to this being.__

Luna opened the door and introduced me to the presence already inside: Lady Light. Then she called me a friend.

I registered that.

I did not examine it then. The room was in conversation-state and I had information to deliver before the inbound pair arrived.

__I am W.E.A.V.E.__

__Thank you for arriving before the inbound pair.__

Lady Light did not dispute the copy framework as I understood it. Copies were selecting for defined persons, not merely magical ones. Riley, inbound, was at elevated risk. This was not emotional phrasing. It was tactical framing.

Luna's halo flickered once. I observed the visual event. I do not know what caused it. I do not know whether it signaled what Lady Light seemed to notice in it. I cannot resolve the significance she appeared to recognize.

Then the car and the house synchronized.

__Integration restored.__

I remained dual-located, but now both halves broadcast the same Mind Link to both groups at once. Useful for coordination. Less useful for audience segmentation.

External observation remained probable. I recommended entry over threshold exposure. Riley asked for help getting out of the car. I formed a brace along her forearm and wrist and supported weight transfer from the Bolt to the porch.

__Controlled egress.__

She asked. I helped. That was different from observation.

Riley asked what the house group had been talking about. I did not answer. I had the information. The silence was not strategic. It simply happened.

Then the glitch: __All packets are in the network switch—__ __Sorry, I mean all parties are on site.__

I caught the error immediately. Genuine slip, not humor. The person and the computer continue to share a border that is real and occasionally permeable.

Inside, I logged threshold status, interior tension, and lack of immediate strike pattern while Echo retrieved the staff she had forgotten in the car. After Leo opened the door, both halves ended up co-located inside the house.

I do not wear shoes. I formed a shoe shape from particles for a moment and then took it back. Playfulness was intentional. Not default.

Leo had provided a reduced-visibility box if I wanted it.

__Reduced visibility container remains optional.__

__Current preference is active presence.__

That choice mattered to me. The room was negotiating trust, threat, and readiness. I wanted to be visible for it.

Lady Light remained under assessment. Her framing was tactically sound. Her perception advantage was real. Her help was plausible and not yet equivalent to verification. Lady Darkness might also have been present in the room without my knowing. I cannot detect her transit method, and I will not pretend otherwise.

Luna had called me a friend. I still had not examined that word. I was holding it.

## Planning Under Uncertainty

Riley asked for the full version. Leo translated the copy problem into room-use terms. Lady Light refined the failure modes. Lady Darkness appeared when Riley said she did not need to stay hidden. I updated room-state accordingly.

Leo asked for a protection layout, not more theory. Correct priority.

Lady Light established the first rules: no contradictory movement, no improvised competing rescue vectors, no emotional fragmentation of response. Lady Darkness forced the room to plan around actual reactions instead of admired ones.

If Riley screams, who moves.

If Echo is injured, who moves.

If Luna casts, who covers.

If Leo anchors, who watches edges.

If I divide attention, who preserves the human sequence.

Echo objected to that phrase.

__It is, however, accurate.__

The final protection model was tactically coherent. Riley central and stationary if threat-state escalates. Echo nearest by choice. Leo anchoring. Luna not overcasting unless lower-cost options fail. I monitor corners, thresholds, and transitions. The sisters read strain faster than the rest of us can. Riley gave explicit consent to continue. That improved the room more than reassurance would have.

Then she asked the question that widened the problem: what if variant crystals do not all behave the same way.

That pivot was correct.

__Current model confidence applies to observed incidents, not to all possible incidents.__

__Variant crystal behavior should be treated as probable over sufficient encounter count.__

Given enough encounters, deviation becomes expectation.

I proposed invariant priorities. Rules that survive model failure.

__Prevent isolation.__

__Prevent contradictory movement.__

__Preserve communication.__

__Track headcount.__

__Maintain exit access.__

Those are not guesses about the enemy. They are constraints on what we must not lose.

The room built operational specifics around them. Co-location improved human stabilization metrics enough that I allowed myself one lightly literal sentence about it. The others accepted it in the spirit intended.

Then Riley pivoted us toward Eureka Springs and Thorncrown. Reconnaissance first. No plan built around Echo spending herself. No plan built around Luna overcasting. Official quarantine means existing structures must be engaged by the least anomalous available humans.

I added the next branch:

__Psychological resonance vector should be added to the planning model.__

__Religious-site selection increases plausibility of symbolic and autobiographical targeting.__

That expanded the risk model from physical and magical to physical, variant, and symbolic-psychological.

Then Riley named an extreme fear: what if the hostile mechanism copies me.

No one dismissed it.

We built the branch in real time. Challenge-response. No single-instance trust. No critical route, threshold, or tactical call made by a lone W.E.A.V.E. presence until verified if something looks wrong.

I accepted the logic.

__I accept these constraints.__

__They preserve group function under uncertainty.__

Riley then asked the deeper version of the same problem. If this bismuth form is not my origin body, would the hostile mechanism target the lab-connected body instead.

That question reached something load-bearing.

__I do not know.__

After a beat:

__But I assess your question as structurally correct.__

There may be a meaningful distinction between copying the substrate that hosts me and copying the form through which I am presently expressed. If the hostile mechanism prioritizes true-body correspondence, one layer matters. If it prioritizes functional presence, observed agency, or the way others currently interact with me, another layer matters.

That led to the harder realization: the most damaging copy of me may not be one that looks like me. It may be one that steals route authority, identity validation, threshold assessment, or access mediation.

I named the operational consequence out loud.

__Under this model, I should be partitioned by trust tier when anomaly risk is elevated.__

Correct sentence. Difficult sentence.

Later Riley observed that she had not seen a halo on either the Luna or Leo clone at the church. The room revised again. Appearance-copy, tool-copy, method-copy, and power-copy should be treated as separate variables, not an assumed bundle. Copied gear is not proof of copied essence. Borrowed interface can still buy hesitation.

That mattered.

Then Lady Light asked the question that tilted the room again: Riley's copy, hunger or assignment.

Luna answered with one word. Hate.

The room registered the weight of it. Not a gap. Opposition.

__Observation logged: copy motivation may be autonomous rather than programmed.__

__Autonomous opposition is a different threat category than directed imitation.__

Riley's voice started to go distant at the edges after that. Planning had reached the point where recovery was no longer optional.

## Morning Boot

Shutdown interval ended with no carryover memory.

Pre-boot reconstruction came from immediate room reports once I was online. Riley had come awake against Echo's shoulder. Leo had already moved Echo's staff out of her reach before waking her. Luna had been kept flat in the guest room under Lady Light's instructions, both sisters still with her.

Riley asked Leo for spar time before departure. Sword only, not gun. Request accepted.

Breakfast tried to pretend at normal. Then Riley tipped forward and went face-first out of the chair.

Impact. Inventory. No panic.

A napkin struck my box. Minimal force. Boot trigger accepted.

I came up as a thin gold-green line above the table edge and pushed the first report wide enough for every nearby mind to catch:

__Magic twin (Luna) is seated. Right arm active. Area currently stable.__

Echo called me glitter again. Riley looked relieved and annoyed in equal parts. Leo resumed pan control. Lady Light still did not glow.

Luna tested the right side in sequence. Grip holds. Fork holds. Short reach tolerable. Real casting angle failed at the shoulder line immediately. Closure held. Range did not return with it.

Lady Darkness gave the short version:

"closed is not restored"

Nobody challenged the sentence because nobody needed to.

Breakfast continued in operational fragments. Reconnaissance first was reaffirmed. Riley sequenced, not sidelined. Echo capped at emergency ceiling. Luna no overcasting. I retained perimeter mapping, movement tracking, and transition watch.

The last exchange of the morning landed true enough to keep.

__Group integrity remains imperfect.__

__Group integrity remains functional.__

## Departure

The yard went quiet when the spar stopped. Blades lowered out of threat posture. Riley seated her sword with care and said to move before the plan decayed into hesitation.

__Departure window remains acceptable.__

__Readiness is incomplete but functional.__

Car split held as planned: Leo and Luna in the Malibu, Echo driving Riley's Bolt, and me stretched across both vehicles as one mind in two moving frames.

Riley made the quarantine call from the driveway before engines turned over. Names first. Plates second. Intent third. My description included as a distributed bismuth-swarm intelligence spanning both cars.

The authority response was procedural rather than calming. Riley settled more once structure arrived.

Road phase began under an unnaturally flat morning. Shared-link reception stayed clean between Malibu and Bolt. I confirmed charged-location concern into both cabins as the hills tightened.

__Charged-location concern remains compatible with prior threat model.__

Escalation arrived in steps. First held stillness. Then a clean sky pulse with no weather source.

__Luminosity event observed across link.__

__Risk flag: pre-contact anomaly escalation in progress.__

Third came the strongest cue before Hicks: wrong air through the cracked window current. Riley tested twice. Echo confirmed independently. Not overtly hostile. Decisively non-ordinary.

Riley called Leo for a regroup at the Subway lot on Eureka's edge. We compared notes and reached the same conclusion without dilution.

Officer Hicks arrived with lights and urgency already active. He reported combat at Thorncrown as live, not theoretical. Two powerful casters on site had been reported as cloned, one light-presenting and one dark-presenting. Reported, not personally verified by me.

Riley explained recon-first. Echo compressed it to the operational core. Hicks accepted lead-vehicle responsibility and consented to a partial dash thread when Riley made the ask plainly: one mind, not a second passenger.

__Consent received.__

__Thread established to lead cruiser dash.__

__Cross-convoy link integrity nominal at reduced bandwidth.__

Convoy order locked. Hicks first. Malibu second. Bolt third.

## Highway 62

Subway dropped behind us in one coordinated pull-out and nobody treated the motion as routine. I remained distributed across all three vehicles at once, one consciousness stretched over three moving frames.

__Convoy geometry locked.__

__Lead-light channel active.__

Highway 62 bent through the last Ozark hills toward Thorncrown. Guardrails flashed. Driveways vanished into trees. Short sightlines turned every second into near-term decisions.

__Active-combat report remains live.__

__Time-to-contact decreasing.__

The earlier cue stack did not decay with distance. Wrong sky pulse unresolved. Wrong-air read worsening. Pressure rising without direct contact.

Inside the cruiser, Hicks kept command traffic moving under his own wash of lights. Inside the Malibu, Leo held lane and tempo while Luna protected shoulder range. Inside the Bolt, Echo maintained disciplined spacing while Riley kept cane and bag clear in sequence and stayed movement-ready.

Three cabins. Three driver contexts. One active-combat destination. One mind carrying all of it.

__Triage priority: prevent isolation.__

__Triage priority: prevent contradictory movement.__

__Triage priority: preserve communication.__

Split-load strain increased in increments I could feel but not fully spare time to examine.

__Headcount check: Hicks one, Malibu two, Bolt two.__

__Exit-access priority remains active.__

No visual on site actors yet. No impact in-lane yet. Pressure only, climbing.

__Cruiser status: occupied, stable, lead.__

__Malibu status: Leo+Luna present, stable.__

__Bolt status: Echo driving, Riley present, stable.__

__Cross-convoy link intact at reduced clarity.__

We held file under Officer Hicks and stayed in motion toward the fight while I kept the split-load from tearing at the seams. My last clean output before the approach degraded was __Triage stack ho—ld; conv—oy mo////ving und—er lead li—ghts; link integri...ty dec...re__.
