Side Moments is a space for non-canon work that can move fast, change shape, or disappear entirely. It collects short stories, prototypes, and experimental fragments that are free to break from the main timeline.

Some posts here may be password-protected while they are being tested, revised, or kept intentionally out of view. Expect the unexpected — this shelf is meant to stay flexible.
